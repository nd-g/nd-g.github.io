Hi everyone! This page is about the scope of the DateaDeafie project, what it is now and what it hopes to become!

What is DateaDeafie?

As of right now DateaDeafie is a general directory for local Deaf events. However it is also a general project to make the Deaf dating and dating in the Deaf world/while Deaf easier! This means it is going to become an ASL based dating app where our accessability needs are adequately met in a dating space. This means that we will strive to create an experience that is uniquely Deaf-based.


This page will be regularly updated to log what steps have been taken on the project and will update more if we find funding.
